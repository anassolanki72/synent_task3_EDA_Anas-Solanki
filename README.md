Netflix Exploratory Data Analysis

About the Project
In this project, I analyzed the Netflix dataset to understand trends in content, including types of shows, growth over time, popular genres, and country-wise distribution. The goal was to derive meaningful insights using data analysis and visualization.

Dataset
Source: Kaggle Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset
File used: netflix_titles.csv

What I Did

Data Cleaning
Handled missing values in country and rating.
Converted date_added into datetime format.
Extracted year_added for trend analysis.
Simplified country information for better analysis.
Analysis
Compared number of movies and TV shows.
Analyzed content growth over the years.
Identified top content-producing countries.
Explored most common genres.
Studied distribution of ratings.
Analyzed movie durations separately from TV shows.

Key Insights
Netflix content increased significantly after 2015.
Movies are more common than TV shows.
USA is the leading content producer.
Drama and international genres are highly popular.
Most movies fall in the 90 to 120 minutes range.

What I Learned
Handling real-world datasets, extracting insights, and thinking from a business perspective while analyzing data.
